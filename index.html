FAQ

  • How many team members can I invite?

    You can invite up to 5 team members if they aren't already part of a team.

  • What is the maxium file upload size?

    No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

  • How do I reset my password?

    Click the customer support icon on the bottom right. The first drop-down will say reset password.

  • How do I cancel my subscription?

    Go to your account homepage on the top right and click account support.

  • Do you provide additional support?

    Head to customer support on the bottom left of our webpage. Contact our team through email or phone. You can also create a ticket.
